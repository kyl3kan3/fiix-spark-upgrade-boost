# A free, branded portal for customers to report problems

> Standard requests and an urgent lane — submissions land straight in your inbox.

Canonical URL: https://maintenease.com/solutions/maintenance-request-portal

Every MaintenEase account ships with a branded public request portal at your-team.maintenease.com/r/your-name. Share the link on your website, print a QR code on the wall, or email it to tenants. Submitters pick a standard request or hit the red Urgent button, fill in a short form, and your team is notified — no app install, no login required.

## Benefits

- **Catch urgent issues in seconds** — Urgent submissions fire instant push and email alerts to your on-call admins and managers — even outside business hours.
- **Stop drowning in email and texts** — Every request lands in one structured inbox with location, photos, and contact info. No more screenshots in group chats.
- **Look professional** — The portal carries your logo and company name. Customers experience a real product, not a Google Form.

## Features

- **Standard + urgent lanes** — Two clear buttons up top. The urgent lane is visually distinct and routes differently.
- **One-click convert to work order** — Triage in the inbox, then convert any request into a fully populated work order with location, contact info, and the right priority.
- **Branded with your logo** — Your colors and logo appear at the top. Use a custom URL slug so the link reads naturally on your website.
- **Public — no login required** — Submitters fill the form in 30 seconds. No account, no install, no friction.
- **Spam-safe by design** — Length-capped fields and per-tenant rate limiting keep the inbox clean without captchas in front of real users.
- **Free on every plan** — Included with every MaintenEase plan — no extra cost per submission.

## Who it's for

- Property managers receiving tenant maintenance requests
- Facility teams collecting issues from staff across multiple buildings
- HOAs and condo associations fielding resident reports
- Schools and clinics where staff need a fast way to flag problems

## FAQ

### Is the portal really free?

Yes. The public request portal is included on every MaintenEase plan, with no per-submission charges.

### Can I customize the URL?

Yes — each company picks a unique slug (e.g. /r/acme-facilities). You can change it any time from settings.

### How do urgent alerts work?

When someone selects the Urgent option, MaintenEase fires push notifications and emails to admins and managers immediately — using their existing notification preferences.

### Do submitters need a MaintenEase account?

No. The portal is fully public. Anyone with the link can submit a request.
