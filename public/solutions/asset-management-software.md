# Asset Management Software that turns your asset register into a working system

> Register, schedule, maintain, and report on every asset — in one place.

Canonical URL: https://maintenease.com/solutions/asset-management-software

MaintenEase is asset management software built for the teams who actually maintain the assets. Register every asset with its location, warranty, parts, and manuals. Layer PM schedules and work orders directly on top. Report on cost, downtime, and lifecycle — per asset, per location, per category. Your asset register stops being a spreadsheet and starts being the system of record for everything that happens to your equipment.

## Benefits

- **One source of truth** — Every asset, every work order, every PM, every part — in one place. No more reconciling spreadsheets at month-end.
- **Smarter capital decisions** — See total cost of ownership and downtime per asset to decide what to repair, replace, or retire.
- **Audit-ready in one click** — Insurance, regulatory, and internal audits export from the asset register with full history attached.

## Features

- **Full asset register** — Capture make, model, serial, warranty, purchase price, installation date, and custom fields per asset class.
- **PM scheduling per asset** — Time-based, runtime-based, or meter-based PMs attached directly to the asset. Work orders generate automatically.
- **Work orders and parts** — Every repair, inspection, and part swap is recorded against the asset — building a complete service history.
- **Warranty and contract tracking** — Alerts before warranties and service contracts expire so you never miss a recoverable repair.
- **Lifecycle and TCO reporting** — Cost per asset, downtime per asset, repair frequency, and depreciation curves — exportable for finance.
- **Role and location permissions** — Restrict each user to the sites, asset classes, or actions they're responsible for.

## FAQ

### How is this different from a CMMS?

Asset management is one of the core capabilities of a CMMS. MaintenEase is a full CMMS — asset management, work orders, PMs, parts, and reporting — sold under whichever name fits how you think about the problem.

### Can I track depreciation?

Yes. Purchase price, installation date, and useful life are captured per asset; straight-line depreciation reports are built in, and you can export raw data for your finance system.

### Does it work for non-physical assets?

Yes — software licenses, certifications, and contracts can be tracked alongside physical assets with their own renewal alerts.
